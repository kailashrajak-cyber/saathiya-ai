Privacy Policy — Saathiya AI

Last updated: June 19, 2026

Saathiya AI ("the App", "we", "us") is a supportive chatbot designed to help teenagers (ages 13–18) with general health, body, and emotional wellbeing questions in simple Hindi/Hinglish. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App handles and how.

In short: We do not create user accounts, we do not ask for your name, and we do not permanently store your conversations on our own servers. Your typed messages are sent securely to our AI provider solely to generate a reply.

1. Information We Collect

The App does not require sign-up and does not knowingly collect personal identifiers such as your name, phone number, email address, school, or location. The only data the App processes is the text you type into the chat box, in order to generate a relevant response.

2. How Your Messages Are Used

When you send a message, it is forwarded — along with the recent messages in your current chat session — to our AI service provider (Anthropic, the maker of Claude) for the sole purpose of generating a reply. We do not use your messages for advertising, and we do not sell or rent your information to anyone.

3. Data Storage and Retention

Saathiya AI does not maintain its own database of user conversations. Your chat history exists only in your device's browser memory for the duration of your session and is cleared when you close or refresh the app. Messages sent to our AI provider are subject to that provider's own data handling and retention practices; you can review Anthropic's privacy policy at anthropic.com/legal/privacy.

4. Crisis-Support Keyword Detection

To help keep users safe, the App scans messages locally for certain words associated with self-harm or crisis situations. If detected, the App displays supportive messaging and helpline numbers (such as iCall and KIRAN). This detection happens within the App itself, is not used to identify or track individual users, and is not reported to any third party or authority.

5. Children's and Teens' Privacy

Saathiya AI is designed for use by teenagers aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from any user, including those under 18, beyond the chat text needed to generate a response. The App does not request or store names, contact details, or precise location at any point. We encourage users who are minors to use this App with the awareness and support of a parent, guardian, or trusted adult, especially when discussing sensitive topics.

6. Not a Substitute for Professional Care

Saathiya AI provides general educational information only. It does not diagnose medical or mental health conditions and is not a replacement for advice from a qualified doctor, counselor, parent, or guardian. If you are experiencing a medical emergency or a mental health crisis, please contact a doctor, trusted adult, or a crisis helpline immediately.

7. Cookies and Analytics

The current version of the App does not use advertising cookies or third-party analytics/tracking SDKs. If this changes in the future, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly.

8. Data Security

Messages are transmitted over secure HTTPS connections. While no system can guarantee absolute security, we take reasonable measures to protect data in transit and do not store sensitive personal data on our servers.

9. Your Choices

Because the App does not create accounts or store conversation history server-side, there is no stored personal data to request, export, or delete. Simply closing the App clears your local session.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
